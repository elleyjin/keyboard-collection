Keydous NJ80

Its plain white plastic case and 75% layout could be mistaken for something from the '90s at a glance, especially if you remove the splashes of color on the space bar, Enter, and Escape keys and go with the white replacements that come in the box. This keyboard uses premium stabilizers that are pre-lubed from the factory, which eliminates the rattling that you get when pressing larger keys. Directly under the switches is a full brass plate, offering extra weight and stiffness, with a layer of sound- and vibration-damping foam between the plate and the circuit board. Under the circuit board is a second layer of foam, further insulating the sound and vibration from the case itself.

Royal Kludge H81

This 75% Layout keyboard uses a gasket material on the keyboard plate's top and bottom edges without being in direct contact with the case, that reduces the occurrence of vibrations, creating a satisfying typing experience as much quieter, softer and cushioned. With its 3750mAh high-capacity battery, it can easily go through 7 days of daily use. Supports 18 RGB Backlight Modes, 8 monochrome Backlight Modes, 1 full Backlight and 16 million colors on individually backlit keys.

Zuoya GMK67

This keyboard is not only elegant and stylish, but also loaded with features and functions that make it one of the most flexible and customizable keyboards in the market. GMK67 mechanical keyboard is a compact 66-key keyboard. It has a trimode connection that lets you use it with USB Type-C, Bluetooth, or 2.4Ghz wireless modes. The keyboard has a poron gasket structure that provides a cushion between the plate and the case. This reduces the vibration and noise of the keyboard and creates a more comfortable and smooth typing feel. The poron gasket also helps to improve the sound quality of the keyboard by enhancing the acoustics.
